The Martian: Examining Human Physical Judgments Across Virtual Gravity Fields

VR, Human Perception, Mental Simulation, Intuitive Pysics

Role
Project Leader
Task
We set up four experiments conducted in an immersive virtual environment to study how humans adapt to new environments with unknown gravitational acceleration.
Results
1. Humans predict accurate parabolic trajectories under different gravities.
2. Humans’ timeestimation in an earth environment fluctuates around the ground truth, although time estimation in unknown gravityfields indicates a bias towards earth’s gravity.
Publication
Accepted by IEEE Virtual Reality 2016, Conditional accepted by IEEE Transections on Virtualization and Computer Graphics (TVCG)
